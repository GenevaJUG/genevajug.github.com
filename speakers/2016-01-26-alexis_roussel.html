Alexis Roussel


Alexis holds a Master in New Technology public law from Paris 10 university. He served as E-Governance specialist for the United Nations. He participated in the World Summit on the Information Society in 2003 and 2005. He joined RWE Switzerland in 2008, an energy trading company, as one of the managers of the Back-Office.

He was the President of the Swiss Pirate Party promoting a human-centric and distributed approach of a technological society.

He is Founder of BITY, a Swiss bitcoin broker company. He is involved in the swiss crypto-currencies regulatory discussions as member of the board of the industry association DFCA.ch.
